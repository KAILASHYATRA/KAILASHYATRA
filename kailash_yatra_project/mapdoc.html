Mount Kailash Outer Kora and Inner Kora Map

The parikrama (kora) of Mount Kailash is for 52 kms out of which about 10 kms is done by vehicle. The outer kora / parikrama of Mount Kailash is a 42 kms trek from Tarboche to Dirapuk (12 kms), Dirapuk to Zuthulpuk (22 kms) and Zuthulpuk to Chongdo (08 kms). When it comes to Inner Kora of Mount Kailash one need to understand that the inner parikrama (kora) has three important legs (1) Charan Sparsh from Dirapuk (06 kms trek), Kuber Kund Trek from Shiva Tsal to Zuthulpuk (18 kms trekking) and the most important part is Nandi Kora which starts from Darchen and includes Nandi Parvat Kora, Ashtapad Trekking, Atma Linga and Saptarishi Caves mainly.
