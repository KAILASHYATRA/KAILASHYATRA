Places to Visit Near Kailash Mansarovar

The majestic Mount Kailash is one of the most passionately followed gigantic peaks in Asia and is not only a religious epicetre for the Hindus, but also for jains, Buddhists and the Bon Pons. Standing upright at a literally high elevation of 6638 m (2177 ft.), the trek to kailash Parvat i.e the Kailash manasarovar Yatra is known as one of the toughest trails in the world.
